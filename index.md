# Sy Nguyen

PhD Researcher in Robotics & Biomechanics  

---

## 👋 About Me
I am a PhD researcher working on human gait analysis using wearable IMU sensors and deep learning.

My research focuses on detecting gait events and predicting push-off timing to support assistive technologies such as exoskeletons and prosthetic devices.

---

## 🔬 Research Overview
- IMU-based gait analysis  
- Deep learning for time-series data  
- Push-off phase detection  
- Assistive robotics  

---

## 🎯 Research Goal
To enable accurate, real-time gait phase detection for intelligent assistive devices that improve mobility, symmetry, and energy efficiency.
